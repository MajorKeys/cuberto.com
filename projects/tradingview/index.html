TradingView

The challenge

Over the last 5 years, Cuberto has formed an intimate relationship with TradingView in a successful attempt to help their team create (and update) the design of their online interfaces. The challenge with this project lay in keeping TradingView at the forefront of upcoming design trends—because TradingView can’t afford to stand still as a business, they can’t afford to slow down on the development of new applications and features for traders either.

The project

TradingView is the best, most powerful and easy to use charting service. Using a unique, trendsetting interface, TradingView gives traders the ability to do their job free of the clutter found on more commonly used, outdated trading platforms. Although TradingView was a complicated, it enabled us to improve its talent at trading interface design—a necessity in order to win new clients in this space.
