Raiffeisen Bank

The challenge

Raiffeisen Bank — one of the most expansive banks in Europe and Russia reached out our agency to rebuild their ATM interfaces. The task was to make it more user-friendly and more up-to-date with current technology. Since thousands of people use these ATMs every day, we had to design an innovative system that was structurally flawless and simple to navigate.

The project

We started from ground zero to completely rebuild the user interface. We redesigned every screen and icon to make it more intuitive for the user. We updated colors and fonts to make them more modern and sophisticated. These systems will survive through decades of technological advancements—and we are proud to put our name on such a prestigious project.
