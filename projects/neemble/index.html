Neemble

The challenge

A Russian-based startup, Neemble, asked us to help them engineer a responsive website that could help them deliver on their mission - to give small and medium enterprises simple, powerful tools to effectively manage their operations. The website need to communicate Neemble’s story, convey their value as a business, and stand-apart in a saturated mobile acquisition market.

The project

Neemble has a distinct advantage over its competition - it's simple, powerful POS system that accepts both cash and credit, includes a cloud-based ERP - system, and features a high-performance CRM to manage client data. We illustrate this, we engineered a responsive website that not only demonstrates Neemble’s distinct advantages over older, legacy systems, but looks great whether viewing on mobile or desktop.
