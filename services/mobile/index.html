Average

project cost

Like web development, the costs associated with mobile development vary — the more functionality required, the longer the mobile development time and the greater the cost. Even though Cuberto bills at a fixed 65 USD per hour, a “from-scratch” mobile development project could take anywhere from two to six months, depending upon the platform (Android vs iOS), UI/UX development, testing, quality assurance, analytic analysis, and branding required. This means an end-to-end mobile development project could cost as much as 50,000 USD.
